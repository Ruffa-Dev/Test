A new cloud for the hive-minded

Hive is a new distributed, peer-to-peer cloud platform. Think of your data as a jigsaw puzzle. Each piece of your puzzle is encrypted with a key and then stored securely by other Hive users. Only you will have access to your jigsaw pieces. This way, no single place has ever access to your entire jigsaw puzzle, that is, your data.

Hive is for everyone

We're on a mission to make data storage and computing distributed, secure, accessible and affordable for everyone — all while reducing its impact on the planet.

The hive five

Here are the 5 principles that make hive thrive.

We are safe

Think of hive as a safe space for all your data.
(What happens on hive, stays on hive)

We respect
your privacy

We believe your data is for your eyes only.
(Don’t worry, no one else will get a peek)

We’re
hive-minded

We seek to harness the power of community.
(Nothing good is ever built alone)

We’re
distributed

We use peer-to-peer technology to create a 
secure, private and affordable network.
(No ads, no scanning, no selling your data)

We’re
sustainable

By storing your data with peers much closer to you, we reduce the need for needless long distance data transfer.
(This makes hive less of a burden on the environment)
