Did you Know?

Hive is a secure, distributed, sustainable cloud, federating and weaving unused capacity of computers.

Did you Know?

When you put your files in HiveDrive, they are split into chunks then compressed, encrypted and encoded for data reliability and distributed over the HiveNet. The reverse happens when you download your data from HiveNet.

Did you Know?

Hive is resilient to nodes disappearing from HiveNet. When your file is split into chunks, HiveDrive builds additional chunks and distributes them along with the original chunks. It only needs to retrieve 50% of them to reconstruct the file.

Did you Know?

There are approximately 20 million servers in data centers around the world. They consume today approximately 40 TWh representing 2% of overall electricity demand. This is expected to grow to up to 20% in the coming decade.
